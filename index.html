— What's Yours, As A Member —

More than a monthly pickup.

Every membership comes with the full set. Read it twice — this is the part most people miss.

I.

Permanent Retail Pricing

Bottle-shop prices on every bottle you drink on-site. No restaurant markup — ever.

II.

Complimentary Flights

A free wine flight, on the house, the third Wednesday of every single month.

III.

$15 Members-Only Flights

Every other Wednesday. A weekly excuse to taste something new at a fraction of the price.

IV.

10% Off To-Go Bottles

Take any bottle from the bottle shop home and save ten percent — year-round, no limit.

V.

15% Off On Pickup Days

Stack the savings. On pickup days, every bottle in the shop is fifteen percent off for members.

VI.

First Access — Allocated Wines

When something rare arrives, members hear about it first. Before it lists. Before it disappears.

VII.

First-Ticket Access — Events

Wine dinners, producer pours, harvest releases. Members get first crack at every seat.

VIII.

A Standing Invitation

This isn't a transaction. It's your club. Wander in, pull up a stool, ask what we're drinking.

Wine is meant to be a fabric of the every day — not just a celebration.
